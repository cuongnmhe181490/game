# Expansion Roadmap

## Muc dich

Tai lieu nay la ban do dai han cho viec mo rong `Nhat Niem Khai Tong` sau khi base game pham gioi da hoan chinh.
Day la tai lieu `planning`, khong phai danh sach tinh nang da co trong build hien tai.

No khong phai pitch lore chung chung. No dung de:

- khoa thu tu mo rong hop ly
- giu cho cac arc sau khac nhau ve fantasy va gameplay
- nhac agent sau nay khong nhoi noi dung chua chin vao build hien tai
- giu `Thanh Huyen Mon la trung tam` o moi giai doan

## Endpoint hien tai

Base game hien tai da hoan chinh mot mach pham gioi:

- Chuong 1-4
- 3 ending:
  - `Chinh Dao Trung Lap`
  - `Ba Dao Xung Ton`
  - `Ngoai Dao Khai Thien`
- diem ket hien tai la `ket thuc mot arc lon`, khong phai `da het moi noi dung tuong lai`

Sau ending, game nen cho cam giac:

- mot thoi dai cua `Thanh Huyen Mon` trong pham gioi da duoc xac lap
- hau qua cua dao lo da mo ra chan troi moi
- the gioi lon hon dang cho, nhung khong duoc pha vo tinh tron ven cua base game

## Trieu ly mo rong

### 1. Moi arc phai mo ra mot fantasy moi

Khong mo rong bang cach:

- tang cap chi so
- tang size ban do
- nhan so luong faction
- them realm moi nhung van choi y chang

Phai mo rong bang cach:

- doi loai ap luc
- doi quy mo quyet dinh
- doi nghia cua viec `lam chu tong mon`
- doi cach nguoi choi tuong tac voi dao thong, inheritance, faction, va the gioi

### 2. Tong mon van la trung tam

Du len Linh gioi hay Tien gioi, game van phai la:

- van hanh mot the luc song
- giu nguoi, giu co nghiep, giu ne nep
- chon huong di cua ca tong mon

Khong duoc bien game thanh:

- action RPG ca nhan
- open world phi luu lanh quanh
- grand strategy truu tuong mat chat tong mon

### 3. Thiên Khư Biến van la truc lien tuc

Moi arc sau phai noi tiep mot trong nhung truc nay:

- hau qua cua cuong hanh phi thang
- vet nut giua cac tang gioi
- vai tro cua `Van Tuong Linh Kinh`
- su that ve dao thong cu va cach no bi tha hoa
- cau hoi: `dieu gi xung dang ton tai sau mot he trat tu that bai`

## Thu tu arc de xuat

## Arc 1: Base Game Pham Gioi

- Trang thai: da hoan chinh
- Core fantasy:
  - song sot
  - phuc hung tong mon
  - mo chan tuong
  - chon dao lo ket cuoc
- Vai tro cua tong mon:
  - tu tan tich thanh mot tieu tong co ten tuoi

## Arc 2: Hau Ket Pham Gioi Va Nguong Hoa Than

- Muc tieu:
  - noi ket cuc pham gioi sang hau qua that su
  - lam ro `Nguyen Anh khong phai dich den`
  - dua nguoi choi toi nguong Hoa Than ma khong mo Linh gioi qua som
- Core fantasy:
  - giu mot tong mon vua lon len truoc suc ep vuot tam vung
  - xu ly hau qua cua ending da chon
  - cham vao nguong `vuot khoi logic pham gioi`
- Core pressure:
  - linh mach va phap tac bat dau khong chiu noi tong mon theo logic cu
  - phe phai cap cao hon khong con xem Thanh Huyen Mon la chuyen dia phuong
  - de tu chu luc va truong lao khong con chi can tai nguyen, ma can `duong len`
- Main narrative question:
  - Thanh Huyen Mon se mang ending cua minh len mot tang gioi moi nhu the nao ma khong tu pha?
- Khac base game o dau:
  - khong con la `phuc hung`
  - la `giu duoc ban sac khi pham vi va cai gia thay doi`

## Arc 3: Linh Gioi Tien Tram

- Core fantasy:
  - dung chan o mot tang gioi moi, noi tong mon khong con nam tren san nha quen thuoc
  - xay mot `linh gioi outpost` hoac `dao son tien tram`
  - hoc cach song voi phap tac, tai nguyen, va the luc cao hon
- Core pressure:
  - moi truong khong hop pham gioi
  - luat choi ngoai giao va tai nguyen thay doi
  - tong mon co the bi nghien nat neu mang nguyen mo hinh cu len
- Main narrative question:
  - Thanh Huyen Mon co the song duoc o tang gioi cao hon ma khong bien thanh ban sao suy doi cua cac the luc lon?
- Sect fantasy:
  - tu `tieu tong co can co` thanh `mot dao thong dang dong coc o bien gioi moi`

## Arc 4: Tien Gioi Dao Thong Tranh Hanh

- Core fantasy:
  - khong chi sinh ton trong trat tu co san, ma tranh quyen dinh nghia trat tu
  - dao thong, su phap, va chinh danh xung dot truc dien
- Core pressure:
  - luat cua the gian cao hon
  - immortal lineages / tien dinh / holy lands
  - moi sai lam khong con la mot vung nui sap do, ma la mot he luat bi lay dong
- Main narrative question:
  - dao ma nguoi choi chon co dung de dan dat mot trat tu lon hon khong, hay no chi dung trong thoi loan?
- Sect fantasy:
  - tu mot tong mon song con thanh mot dao thong co kha nang tranh ngoi dinh nghia

## Arc 5: Ngoai Thien / Than Dao / Cuu Cuc

- Trang thai:
  - chi dat dinh huong, chua cam ket lam
- Core fantasy:
  - viet lai quan he giua dao, luat, va su ton tai
- Chi nen lam neu:
  - Arc 2-4 da thanh cong
  - he thong cot loi da duoc stress-test that su
- Rui ro:
  - rat de mat chat `tong mon fantasy`
  - rat de tro thanh cosmology spam

## Long-term realm roadmap

Khung realm de xuat:

### Tang 1: Mortal

- Realm:
  - `Pham The`
  - `Luyen Khi`
  - `Truc Co`
  - `Kim Dan`
  - `Nguyen Anh`
- Vai tro:
  - day la nhom realm cua base game
- Core fantasy:
  - xay nen tong mon, xay can co dao thong
- Trang thai:
  - da choi duoc

### Tang 2: Mortal-Transcendent Threshold

- Realm de xuat:
  - `Hoa Than`
  - co the them 1-2 moc sau do neu can, nhung khong can khoa som
- Vai tro:
  - day la cau noi giua pham gioi va tang gioi cao hon
- Chi nen gioi thieu khi:
  - da co arc hau ending dau tien
  - da ro gameplay pressure moi
- Core unlock:
  - tiep can phap tac / essence / dao luc theo cach ma realm cu khong cho phep
  - mo ra doi hoi moi trong inheritance, exploration, va doctrine

### Tang 3: Spirit

- Realm de xuat:
  - ten cu the de quyet sau implementation arc 3
- Vai tro:
  - realm cua Linh gioi, noi luat choi khong con la pham gioi phong to
- Core unlock:
  - resource va law system moi
  - disciple selection va adaptation pressure moi
  - exploration mang tinh `frontier` va `co che song con o the gioi la`

### Tang 4: Immortal

- Realm de xuat:
  - chi nen dat ten khi da cham arc 4
- Vai tro:
  - realm de tranh dao thong, khong chi tranh tai nguyen
- Core unlock:
  - conflict o cap doctrine / law / legitimacy
  - faction khong chi la to chuc, ma la `lineage of order`

### Tang 5: Transcendent / Divine

- Trang thai:
  - placeholder dai han
- Chi dung de:
  - ngan du an tu khoa minh vao tran Tien gioi
- Khong nen:
  - dat ten day du
  - dua vao data player-facing qua som

## Gameplay expansion philosophy by arc

## Base game

- Survival
- Sect rebuilding
- First doctrine
- Early inheritance truth

## Arc hau ending pham gioi

- Khong mo them 10 subsystem moi
- Nen mo:
  - stronger continuation of ending fallout
  - realm-threshold pressure
  - larger external pressure
  - sect identity under stress at scale

## Linh gioi

- Nen mo fantasy:
  - adaptation
  - outpost building
  - essence/law mismatch
  - selecting who and what the sect can carry upward
- Khong nen chi la:
  - ban do moi
  - quai to hon
  - item hiem hon

## Tien gioi

- Nen mo fantasy:
  - doctrine governance at cosmic scale
  - legal/metaphysical diplomacy
  - lineage conflict
- Khong nen chi la:
  - faction relation version 2 nhung so lon hon

## Divine/external

- Neu co, fantasy phai la:
  - rewrite order, not just survive inside it
- Neu khong co design ro, dung lai o Tien gioi se tot hon hon la mo vo toi va

## Cach cac system hien tai nen scale

## 1. Sect management

- Van la cot song o moi arc
- Ve sau co the mo rong:
  - multiple seats of power
  - outpost / branch management
  - inter-world supply lines
- Khong nen lam som:
  - territory conquest sim
  - dozens of passive admin subpanels

## 2. Disciples

- Van la NPC cot loi o moi arc
- Ve sau co the mo rong:
  - phan loai de tu theo lineage / transmission role
  - succession pressure
  - adaptation to higher-law worlds
- Khong nen lam som:
  - social graph toan tong
  - life sim chi tiet tung nguoi

## 3. Elders / guest cultivators

- Ve sau nen tro thanh:
  - strategic doctrine carriers
  - bridge to higher factions / worlds
- Chua nen som:
  - bureaucracy simulator

## 4. Diplomacy / factions

- Base game: thu, canh bao, trade, pressure
- Ve sau:
  - doctrine blocs
  - world-layer powers
  - legal / metaphysical bargaining
- Khong nen som:
  - war map
  - grand strategy layer

## 5. Exploration

- Base game: map nodes, inheritance spots, boss reward
- Hau ending:
  - larger consequence expeditions
  - threshold zones
  - unstable cross-layer ruins
- Linh gioi:
  - exploration = thiet lap foothold, khong chi loot run
- Khong nen som:
  - roguelike overhaul
  - full procedural dungeon platform

## 6. Inheritance / relics

- Can tang tam quan trong theo arc
- Ve sau nen tro thanh:
  - doctrinal engines
  - keys to higher laws
  - route-specific inheritance burdens
- Khong nen som:
  - hundreds of relic fragments vo nghia

## 7. Inventory / alchemy / artifacts

- Van nen phuc vu tong mon va cultivation
- Ve sau co the mo:
  - higher-order ingredients
  - law-infused crafting
  - sect-scale relic projects
- Khong nen som:
  - loot shower
  - affix system la liet

## 8. Chapter progression / ending alignment

- Van nen la fixed macro spine + weighted state-aware events
- Ve sau:
  - ending alignment cua base game nen tro thanh starting pressure cho arc moi
- Khong nen som:
  - giant branching engine

## Faction/world structure ve sau

## Cap 1: Small sects / regional powers

- Da duoc dung trong base game

## Cap 2: Great sects / holy lands / upper mortal blocs

- Nen mo o arc hau ending pham gioi
- Vai tro:
  - cho nguoi choi thay the cuc lon hon cap vung

## Cap 3: Spirit-world powers

- Nen mo khi vao Linh gioi
- Vai tro:

## Cap 4: Immortal courts / lineage orders

- Nen mo o Tien gioi
- Vai tro:

## Cap 5: External / unknown powers

- Chi nen mo neu can cho climax that su
- Vai tro:
  - dat gioi han cho nhung gi ngay ca Tien gioi cung khong kiem soat duoc

## Sequencing implementation de xuat

1. Cung co types/save cho world-layer va arc-layer
2. Khoa long-term realm tiers o muc data
3. Lam `arc hau ending pham gioi` truoc
4. Chi mo `continue beyond ending` khi arc hau ending da choi duoc dau tien
5. Sau do moi can nhac Linh gioi playable
6. Tien gioi chi nen vao backlog implementation sau khi Linh gioi da tao ra fantasy khac that su

## Nen build som

- hook save / schema
- arc/world metadata
- continuation consequence state
- chapter planning templates

## Nen de muon

- realm moi playable
- world-layer moi playable
- faction classes moi
- law systems / essence systems
- branch-outpost gameplay

## Anti scope-creep

Khong lam qua som:

- 20 realm tiers visible trong build
- Linh gioi xuat hien trong menu khi chua playable
- open world rewrite
- war sim
- combat system overhaul chi vi len tang gioi cao hon
- rewrite tong the current systems truoc khi chung that su bi stress
- lore inflation khong co gameplay fantasy moi di cung

## Cac quyet dinh con mo

Nhung diem nay nen de den luc lam expansion that:

- Hoa Than la `cuoi pham gioi` hay `nguong sang Linh gioi`
- Linh gioi nen mo bang 1 outpost hay 1 migration that su
- Tien gioi co can mot arc rieng hay la culmination cua Linh gioi
- Co nen co `divine/external arc` hay dung lai o xung dot Tien gioi
- Bao nhieu ending fallout can duoc mang sang arc moi theo cach player-facing
